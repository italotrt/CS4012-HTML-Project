What we offer

Helping people who are in need of emergency is what the Drone Care does, and so we want to make sure that we’re there for as many people as we possibly after what has been a tough year for people who are in pain and living on the streets.

We offer a wide range of equipment support in our headquaters (where all the drone's are stored) and charging centres all-round across Ireland. We will be supporting people experiencing chest pains and heart attacks however we can in these pandemic times where people are social distancing.
