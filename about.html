Product

Our product would be an infrastructure of modular drones capable of being quickly loaded with the relevant supplies and dispatched. Modular Drone Infrastructure building on existing 112 system.

The drone could supply:

  • Defibrillators
  • Epi-pens
  • Insulin
  • Inhalers
  • Covid-19 medicine
The drones are capable of carrying less than 5kgs. If the patient is at a local health post, a trained individual applies the treatment, otherwise, instructions can be given over the phone or by the equipment itself. Modern AEDs can talk untrained individuals through assembly and are automatic.
